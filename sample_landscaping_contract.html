This Landscaping Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on by and between:

Landscaper:

1. Scope of Services

The Landscaper agrees to provide general landscaping and lawn maintenance services, which may include mowing, edging, trimming, weed control, leaf removal, planting, mulching, cleanup, and related outdoor maintenance services as agreed by both parties.

2. Service Schedule

Service Type Frequency Notes
Lawn Mowing and Edging Weekly / Biweekly Schedule to be confirmed with Client.
Seasonal Cleanup As Needed Includes leaves, debris, and general yard cleanup.
Mulching / Planting By Request Materials billed separately unless included in quote.

3. Payment Terms

The Client agrees to pay the Landscaper the agreed service fee of $ per service visit, unless otherwise stated in writing. Payment is due within days of invoice receipt.

4. Materials and Equipment

The Landscaper shall provide the basic tools and equipment needed to complete the services. Any additional materials, including plants, mulch, soil, fertilizer, or specialty supplies, may be billed separately with Client approval.

5. Cancellation and Rescheduling

Either party may request cancellation or rescheduling with reasonable notice. Weather-related delays may require services to be rescheduled at the Landscaper’s discretion.

6. Independent Contractor

The Landscaper is acting as an independent contractor and is not an employee of the Client. The Landscaper is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, licenses, and business obligations.

7. Liability

The Landscaper agrees to use reasonable care while performing services. The Client agrees to disclose any known hazards, irrigation systems, buried lines, pets, or property conditions that may affect the work.
